HK AV•Show

check out on live preview

Starting from 2003, the Audiotechnique holds a 3-day international “Hong Kong Hi-End Audio Visual Show” in August each year. The Show is the biggest of its kind in Hong Kong. It attracts representatives from the audio field and audiophiles from China and all over the world including Japan, Korea, Europe and the US. It has been reckoned as one of the three most important Audio Visual Shows worldwide.
